Game Introduction

Explore the wonderful adventure of Pixel Survival World, and learn about this innovative game that combines pixel art style, survival gameplay, and blockchain technology.

Game Story

In a world where digital and physical realities merge, Pixel Survival World emerges as a unique frontier. This blocky universe was created by ancient digital architects who embedded blockchain technology into its very fabric.

As one of the chosen explorers, you've been granted access to this world where every item you craft, every structure you build, and every achievement you earn is truly yours - secured by the power of blockchain.

But beware, this world is not without its challenges. Hostile creatures roam the lands, resources are scarce, and survival requires wit, skill, and collaboration with other players. Will you thrive in this new digital frontier?

Game World

Forest Realm

Lush forests filled with various trees, plants, and wildlife. The perfect starting area for new players to gather initial resources and build their first shelter.

Mountain Heights

Towering mountains rich with rare minerals and ores. Challenging terrain with dangerous cliffs, but rewarding for those brave enough to explore its peaks and caves.

Desert Wastes

Vast deserts with scarce resources but unique treasures. Water management becomes crucial, and ancient ruins hide valuable artifacts and blockchain-powered relics.

Character Classes

Miner

Specialized in resource gathering and mining. Can extract resources more efficiently and discover rare minerals others might miss.

Builder

Masters of construction and design. Can build structures using fewer resources and create special buildings with unique benefits.

Hunter

Skilled in combat and survival. Excels at defeating creatures, crafting weapons, and surviving in harsh environments.

Explorer

Natural pathfinders and discoverers. Can navigate more efficiently, find hidden locations, and identify valuable blockchain assets.

Key Features

True Digital Ownership

All in-game items, land, and achievements are tokenized on the blockchain, giving players true ownership of their digital assets. Trade, sell, or showcase your items across the digital ecosystem.

Player-Driven Economy

A thriving marketplace where players can trade resources, items, and land. The economy is entirely player-driven, with value determined by rarity, utility, and demand.

Community Governance

Players can participate in game decisions through a decentralized governance system. Vote on new features, balance changes, and special events to shape the future of the game.

Cross-Platform Play

Play seamlessly across multiple devices. Your progress, items, and achievements are tied to your blockchain identity, not to any specific platform or device.
