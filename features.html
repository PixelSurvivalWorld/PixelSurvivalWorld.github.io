Game Features

Explore the unique features of Pixel Survival World, an innovative game that combines pixel art style, survival gameplay, and blockchain technology.

Survival Gameplay

In Pixel Survival World, you need to gather resources, build shelters, craft tools and weapons to survive in a challenging environment.

  • Resource Collection System: Chop trees, mine ores, gather plants to obtain essential resources for survival.

  • Hunger and Health System: Manage your hunger and health status through hunting, farming, and cooking.

  • Day-Night Cycle: Gather resources during the day, defend against enemies at night, adapt to different challenges.

  • Weather System: Deal with rain, snow, storms, and various weather conditions that affect your survival strategy.

Building System

Express your creativity through our flexible building system. Create anything from simple shelters to complex structures.

  • Block-Based Building: Intuitive building system with various block types and materials.

  • Structure Templates: Save and share your building designs with other players.

  • Building Protection: Secure your structures with blockchain-based ownership verification.

  • Collaborative Building: Work together with friends to create massive structures.

Blockchain Integration

Experience true ownership of your in-game assets through blockchain technology. Every item you earn or create has real value.

  • NFT Items: Unique in-game items are tokenized as NFTs, providing true ownership and tradability.

  • Smart Contracts: Secure trading and ownership verification through blockchain smart contracts.

  • Cross-Game Assets: Use your NFT items across different compatible games and platforms.

  • Marketplace: Buy, sell, and trade your in-game assets in our decentralized marketplace.

Community Features

Join a vibrant community of players and creators. Collaborate, trade, and shape the future of the game together.

  • Guilds System: Form or join guilds to collaborate with other players on large-scale projects.

  • Trading System: Trade resources and items directly with other players in a secure environment.

  • Community Events: Participate in regular community events and competitions with unique rewards.

  • Governance System: Vote on game updates and participate in community-driven development decisions.
